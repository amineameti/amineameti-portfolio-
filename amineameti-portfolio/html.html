About Me

My name is Amine, born in May 2004 in Tetovo, Macedonia, and raised in Ilijaš near Sarajevo, where I finished both middle and high school. I speak three languages — Bosnian (strongest), Albanian, and English.

My biggest passion is photography — capturing and editing moments that tell a story. In the Gallery section, you can explore a collection of my photos, all taken and edited by me. I also enjoy design, having created logos and catalogs for my father’s business, which are also showcased in the Gallery.

Alongside my creative work, I hold certificates in QA Manual Testing, QA Animation Testing, and Web Development. I’m also familiar with UX/UI design. In fact, this website itself is a project I fully designed and developed.

I’m passionate about combining creativity with technical skills — whether through design, development, or photography — to build meaningful and visually engaging projects.
